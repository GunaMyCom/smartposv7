## 🛠️ What You Can Do

- View Hold Bills.
- View today's settled bills.
- View previous date bills.
- Search specific bill by number.
- Review complete bill details.

---

## 🛠️ How to Use

1. Click **LookUp** on billing screen.
2. Use checkboxes:
   - ✅ _Bill No_ → Hold Bills
   - 🕓 _History & Settled Order_ → Past Bills
   - ✅ _Settled Order_ → Today's Bills
3. Enter **Bill Number** to search.
4. Click any bill to see details: items, quantity, price, total.
