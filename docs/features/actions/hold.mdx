## 📝 When to Use

- Customer steps away during billing.
- Need to serve another customer mid-transaction.

---

## 🛠️ How to Use Hold

1. Scan/add items for the customer.
2. Click the **Hold** button.
3. The bill will be saved as "Hold".

---

## 🔁 Retrieve a Hold Bill

1. Click **LookUp**.
2. Hold bills show by default.
3. Select a bill to resume.
4. Add more items if needed.
5. Proceed to payment.
