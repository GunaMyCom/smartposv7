# Price Checker

Check an item’s price without adding it to the bill.

---

## 🎬 Demo Video

![Price Checker Demo](/assets/videos/pricechecker.mp4)

---

## 🛠️ How to Use

1. Click the **Price Checker** button.
2. A new screen appears.
3. Scan the item.

System shows:
- Price
- Item Name
- Name 2

---

## ➕ Optional Actions

- Click **Add to Cart** to include it in the bill.
- Click **Cancel** to discard.
