4. Ideal for **walk-in customers** or when electronic methods are **unavailable**.

## üõ†Ô∏è How to Use

1. Create a new bill by adding all the items the customer wishes to purchase.  
2. On the billing screen, click the **Cash** button.  
3. The application will immediately **process the cash payment** and complete the transaction.  
4. Once the cash payment is successful, the bill will be **automatically saved** in the system.
