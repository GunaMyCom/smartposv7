## ğŸ•’ When to Use

1. The customer returns items **without the original invoice**.

## ğŸ› ï¸ How to Use

1. Click the **Credit Note** button on the billing screen.  
2. Select **Without Bill Credit Note** from the options.  
3. Click **Create Credit Note**.  
4. **Scan or search** the item(s) being returned.  
5. Enter the correct **quantity and price** for each item.  
6. **Review** all item details for accuracy.  
7. Click **Save** (on the bottom keyboard) to complete the credit note.
