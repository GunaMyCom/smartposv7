## 🔁 Repeat Button

- Doubles the quantity of the **last added item**.

### Example:
- Qty 1 → Click → 2 → Click → 4 → Click → 8

---

## 🔄 Update Item Button

- Adjust quantity of **any item**.

### Steps:
1. Select the item.
2. Click **Update Item**.
3. Adjust quantity using ➕ or ➖ buttons.
4. Total price updates automatically.

---

## 🧾 Manually Enter Price

If an item has **no price**, app prompts:
> "Please, update the price."

Steps:
- Enter price → Click **Add to Cart**
