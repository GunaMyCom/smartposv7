## ğŸ› ï¸ How to Use

1. Create a new bill by adding all the items the customer wishes to purchase.  
2. On the billing screen, click the **Credit Card** button.  
3. A new screen will appear showing different card type buttons, such as:
   - Credit Card  
   - Debit Card  
   - Visa  
   - MasterCard  

4. Click the card type the customer wants to use.  
5. The application will **process the payment** and **complete the transaction**.  
6. Once the payment is successful, the bill is **automatically saved**.
